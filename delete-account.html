Request to Delete Your Account and Data

If you would like to delete your user account and all personal data associated with it, you can request this by contacting us directly via email.

How to Request Deletion

Please send an email to bliss.berlin@googlemail.com with the subject line: "Delete My Account" and include your registered email address.

What Happens Next?

  • Your request will be processed within 7 days.
  • All data will be permanently deleted unless legal retention periods apply.
  • You will receive confirmation once the process is complete.

Important Note

We may retain certain minimal records where legally required (e.g. for fraud prevention or tax regulations). All other data will be removed permanently.
